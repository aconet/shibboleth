Explanation

Logging out locally: Your TYPO3 backend session is closed. However, your Shibboleth session is probably still active.

To completely log out of the system, close your browser.

With a Shibboleth session still active, you will be logged in back into the TYPO3 backend, as soon as you navigate to the backend page.
